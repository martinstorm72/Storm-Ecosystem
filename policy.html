PRIVACY POLICY
LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026 // STORM OS // EGYPT

1. INTRODUCTION

Storm OS ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Martin, based in Egypt. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Storm OS platform at any of our domains or deployments.

By using Storm OS, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We take your privacy seriously and will never sell your personal data to third parties.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Data: Your name and email address when you register.
  • Payment Data: Billing information processed by Paddle (our payment provider). We do not store card numbers — Paddle handles all payment data under their own PCI-compliant systems.
  • Usage Data: Your subscription status and payment confirmation stored in our Supabase database.
  • Local Data: All dashboard activity (tasks, habits, sessions, journal entries, analytics) is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to our servers.
  • Technical Data: Basic server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) for security and debugging purposes.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To authenticate your account and verify your subscription status.
  • To process your payment and grant dashboard access upon confirmation.
  • To send transactional emails (payment receipts, account confirmation) via Supabase Auth.
  • To maintain the security of our platform and prevent fraud.
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third parties.

4. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your account data (name, email, subscription status) is stored in Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant cloud database. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest. Payment processing is handled exclusively by Paddle, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified provider.

Your productivity data (tasks, habits, sessions, journal entries, goals, analytics) is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device. We cannot access this data. Clearing your browser storage will permanently delete this data.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • Supabase: Authentication and user profile database. Privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.
  • Paddle: Payment processing. Privacy policy at paddle.com/legal/privacy.
  • OneCompiler: In-browser code execution via embedded iframe. Privacy policy at onecompiler.com. No code you write is stored by us.
  • Google Fonts: Typography delivery. Privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by cancelling your subscription and deleting your account.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at martinsamer91@gmail.com.

7. COOKIES

Storm OS does not use tracking cookies. We use browser localStorage to maintain your dashboard data and Supabase Auth session tokens for authentication purposes only. No advertising or analytics cookies are used.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Storm OS is not directed at anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on the platform. Continued use of Storm OS after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. CONTACT

For privacy concerns or data requests, contact Martin at:
martinsamer91@gmail.com
