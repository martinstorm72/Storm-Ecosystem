⚡ Deep Work — Add tasks, set priorities, track completion & earn XP

🗓️ Monthly Planner — Full calendar view with color-coded categories

📚 Flashcards — Spaced repetition system with smart SRS intervals
🗺️ Course Planner — Track courses, modules, study hours & activity grid
🗂️ Project Portfolio — Log projects with tech stack, GitHub links & resume flag
📓 Field Notes — Operational journal with tags, search & full history
📊 Analytics — 90-day heatmap, peak hours & weekly ops breakdown
🎯 Focus Score — Daily performance grade (S–F) calculated from your activity
☁️ Cloud Sync — Data syncs across all your browsers & devices
